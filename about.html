Who am I?

My name is Avery Chan. I am a linguist and programmer.

My linguistic interests include dialectology, historical linguistics, lexicology. I am fascinated with translation and its intersections with semantics, nuance, humanity, and art.

My other interests include retro computing, photography, puzzle-solving, and reading. I have a passion for exploring my local area and finding beauty and interest in the everyday. (I also love glimpses of daily life in linguistics: for what concepts does a language have vocabulary for?)

I have a soft spot for whimsy. I find that adding a touch of lightheartedness and playful spirit can make even the most mundane times bright.
